Statistics

Data (People)

  • Every 24 hours, 11 people go missing in the whole country.
  • 1,360 people went missing during this present year's first four months.
  • Up to date there are almost 26,000 cases about people whose whereabouts are unknown.
  • Three out of ten people gone missing are women and practically they are young people between 15 and 29 years old.
  • Nuevo Leon is in third place with 2,167 missing people per year.

    • Data (Pets)

  • Approximately 23,000 pets per year are lost and 19,000 of them are dogs.
